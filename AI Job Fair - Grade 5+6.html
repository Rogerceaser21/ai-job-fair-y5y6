WALT · We Are Learning To
  • Identify and examine how A.I. is used across different jobs and places in our city.
  • Describe and explain what each A.I. system or Bot is responsible for and why it is useful.
  • Create a short written reflection on how A.I. helps people do their jobs better.
Success Criteria · How will I know I've got it?
★Getting started: I can use the Teleport Bot to visit a station, read the board, and talk to the expert.
★★Levelling up: I can photograph and name all 5 stations clearly by what each A.I. does.
★★★Going further: I can create a 1 to 2 paragraph quill entry that explains how the world uses A.I. to help people do their jobs better, using examples from 3+ stations.

Need Help? Want a Challenge?

🟢 Support

  • Work with a buddy to teleport.
  • Shorten the quill to 3–4 sentences on 2 stations.
  • Sentence frame: "At the ___, A.I. helps by ___ which means ___".

🔴 Challenge

  • In the quill, compare two stations and justify which job A.I. helps most.
  • Examine a board: find an A.I. use that is NOT a Bot (e.g. Predictive Analytics).
  • Predict one more city job A.I. could help, and how.
Share + Exit Ticket

Show what you discovered.

  • Show a partner ONE station where A.I. surprised you.
  • Volunteers: show a photo and explain what that A.I. does and why it helps.

🎟 Exit Ticket: read one sentence from your quill reflection aloud.
